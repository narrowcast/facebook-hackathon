Shopify App for Facebook Advertising

Advertise your product on Facebook with Shopify.

Concept

1. Connect

Connect your Shopify store to the Vimote Shopify app and Vimote Facebook app. This is done by simply installing the Vimote app from the Shopify App Store.

2. Advertise

Create Facebook Page Post Link Ads directly from your product listing. Offsite conversion pixels for page view and checkout are automatically created and installed.

3. Optimize

Analyze which advertisements are giving you the best return on investment. Targeting and budget are optimized automatically according to the ad performance
